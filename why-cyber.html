🔐 Why I Chose Cybersecurity

Published: July 2025

I chose cybersecurity because I want to protect — not just systems, but people, ideas, and communities who depend on technology every day.

Growing up, I witnessed how fast tech was transforming lives across Africa. But with that growth came new threats — scams, identity theft, data leaks. I knew then: someone had to stand guard. I wanted to be that someone.

Cybersecurity is more than coding. It's about:

  • Thinking like an attacker 👾
  • Solving complex puzzles 🧠
  • Understanding both technology and human behavior

What excites me most is the purpose. It’s not just a career — it’s a mission to build trust and resilience into the digital world.

"I don’t just want to stop cyber threats — I want to build a future that’s safe by design."

My goal? To be the kind of professional that businesses and communities can count on. The kind who leads, who defends, and who helps others grow safely online.

— Shal Rhimba
